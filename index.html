Frequently Asked Questions

There are areas of the body that are more painful, as happens depending on the size of the tattoo. In addition, depending on the level of body fat, the pain will be more or less intense. If the area in which we are going to tattoo is bone, the pain will be more intense, if not, the sensation will be similar to a tingling. In this line, it is important to bear in mind that each person does not bear pain the same.
⠀

In order to evaluate and budget your idea, I need the following information:

Idea about the tattoo (Being as detailed as possible)
Approximate size in centimeters
Place/space on your body where the tattoo will go
If necessary, attach a photo or reference image of both the idea and the area to be tattooed
⠀

It all depends on the approach we want to give it. There is pain, visibility and aging. Three factors that we have to take into account when choosing an area. Along with this, we must start from the fact that there will be areas that have to be reviewed frequently, which can also increase the price that we set ourselves at first.

Before deciding to tattoo a person, we take care of solving any of the doubts, fears or concerns that may arise. It is important that, if you have skin problems, any type of allergy or medical problems, you let us know so that we can offer you the best solution.
