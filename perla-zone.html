How much does it hurt to get a tattoo?

This is the question that all of us who go to get tattoos for the first time ask ourselves. Does it hurt? It depends. We are aware that many people do not get tattoos for fear of pain, but the sensation of the needle on the skin varies in each one. There are areas of the body that are more painful, as happens depending on the size of the tattoo. In addition, depending on the level of body fat, the pain will be more or less intense. If the area in which we are going to tattoo is bone, the pain will be more intense, if not, the sensation will be similar to a tingling. In this line, it is important to bear in mind that each person does not bear pain the same, so until you decide to get a tattoo you will not know what the feeling is that awaits you.

Are there areas to get tattoos that are better than others?

It all depends on the approach we want to give it. There is pain, visibility and aging. Three factors that we have to take into account when choosing an area. Along with this, we must start from the fact that there will be areas that have to be reviewed frequently, which can also increase the price that we set ourselves at first. Our recommendations, despite being something personal, are that you opt for areas that have less visibility, since factors such as the sun or constant friction will cause the tattoo to lose quality over time and that you have to review it again.

What if I have any kind of allergy? Is it something that you have in mind?

Before deciding to tattoo a person, we take care of solving any of the doubts, fears or concerns that may arise. It is important that, if you have skin problems, any type of allergy or medical problems, you let us know so that we can offer you the best solution.
